Dr. Romantic 2

Drama , Romance

Season 2 of Dr. Romantic, tells the story of two young doctors named Cha Eun Jae
and Seo Woo Jin. Cha Eun Jae is a second year pulmonologist. She is a smart and confident doctor. But She has a trauma that makes her always vomit during operations.

Cast : Lee Sung-kyung, Ahn Hyo-seop, Han Suk-kyu, Kim Joo-Hun, Jin Kyung

Creator : Ahn Je-hyun, Shin Sang-yun

Episodes

Episode 01

1 hours 7 minute

Min Guk, who is known as a highly competent surgeon, demonstrating how he operates in front of a group of people. But he faced a crisis during the operation.

Episode 02

1 hours 11 minute

Yong Joo offers Woo Jin a job at Doldam Hospital. Will Woo Jin accept the job offer from Yong Joo and appear at Doldam Hospital?

Episode 03

1 hours 3 minute

On their first day at Doldam Hospital, Woo Jin and Eun Jae accompany Yong Joo in operating on the Minister of National Defense who was seriously injured in a car accident. Will they be able to save the Minister?

Episode 04

1 hours 3 minute

Eun Jae receives a harsh reprimand from Yong Joo for running out of the OR in the middle of surgery. A group of doctors and nurses came to Doldam Hospital unannounced at night.
